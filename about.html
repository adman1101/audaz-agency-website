OUR ORIGIN STORY

From Reagan's campaign to your small business.

1980

Ronald Reagan Presidential Campaign

Rene helps spearhead Latin media outreach alongside Al Cardenas, head of the Latin Campaign in South Florida — a masterclass in audience targeting at the highest level.

1982

Sábado Gigante — US Launch

Rene helps spearhead the most successful Latin television program in US history, hosted by Mario Kreutzberger (Don Francisco). Goes nationwide in months.

1984

Reagan Re-Election Campaign

Returns to build Reagan's historic 49-state landslide campaign. The Latin media strategy becomes a model for political marketing for decades.

1992

The Real World — Season 1, Miami Beach

Rene builds the set for MTV's The Real World Season 1 in Miami Beach — one of the most iconic moments in reality television history.

2026

AUDAZ Agency — Born Bold

After 40+ years building audiences across radio, TV, political media, and digital — Rene and Bobbie launch AUDAZ Agency. Everything learned. Everything earned. Now all in for your small business.
