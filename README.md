## About AUDAZ Agency

**AUDAZ Agency LLC** is a full-service bilingual (English/Spanish) marketing agency based in South Florida, serving small businesses nationwide and internationally.

We bring **Fortune 500 expertise to small businesses** — combining 40+ years of media, marketing, and brand-building experience with cutting-edge digital strategy.

### Our Story
Founded by **Rene Galan** (Co-Founder & Chief Growth Officer) and **Bobbie Galan** (Principal), AUDAZ was built on a simple belief: small businesses deserve the same bold, world-class marketing that big brands take for granted.

Rene's career spans over four decades — from helping spearhead Ronald Reagan's presidential campaigns (1980 & 1984) alongside Al Cardenas, to launching the most successful Latin television program in US history (*Sábado Gigante*), to working with Fortune 500 brands including AT&T, Adidas, Yeti Coolers, Google Fiber, Dell, and Casio.

Bobbie brings 40 years of medical practice management and operational excellence — the discipline and leadership that makes AUDAZ run like a well-oiled machine.

**We don't do quiet.**
